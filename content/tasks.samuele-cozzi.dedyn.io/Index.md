# Vikunja

Vikunja is an open-source, self-hostable task and project manager for organizing to-dos, lists, and team work. It supports views like list, Kanban, Gantt, and table, and you can run it on your own infrastructure or use its hosted cloud option.[vikunja](https://vikunja.io/convince/)

## What it does

Vikunja is designed to replace tools like Todoist, Trello, or Asana for people or teams that want more control over their data. Tasks are its core building block, and they can include descriptions, labels, priorities, assignees, comments, and checklists.[vikunja](https://vikunja.io/help/tasks/)

## Why people use it

A big reason people choose Vikunja is privacy and control: it is open source under AGPLv3 and can be self-hosted, so your data stays on your server if you want. It also supports collaboration features for shared projects and team workflows.[vikunja](https://vikunja.io/)
